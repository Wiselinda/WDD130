Overview

Purpose

This website serves as the online presence for our exciting rafting adventures. Explore, book, and join us for an unforgettable experience!

Audience

  • Who are the target customers?
    Adventure enthusiasts aged 18-40, seeking outdoor experiences. This includes thrill-seekers, nature lovers, and those looking for unique recreational activities.
  • What do they want?
    Customers want an exhilarating rafting experience that combines excitement with the beauty of nature. They also seek convenience in booking options and clear information about the rafting packages.
  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by accessing the website?
    Customers expect information on rafting routes, difficulty levels, safety measures, and clear instructions for booking, including availability, pricing, and reservation options.
