Explore Our Rafting Trips

Choose from a variety of exciting rafting adventures.

Trip: White Water Adventure

Description: Embark on an adrenaline-fueled journey down the roaring rapids of the Nile River. Our White Water Adventure trip is perfect for thrill-seekers looking for an exhilarating experience. Navigate through challenging rapids while surrounded by stunning natural landscapes. No prior rafting experience required.

Duration: 2 days

Difficulty: Intermediate

Price:$150 per person

Includes:Rafting equipment, exprienced guide, lunch

Trip: Sunset Cruise

Description: Experience the beauty of the Nile River at dusk with our Sunset Cruise trip. Relax and unwind as you sail along the tranquil waters, witnessing the breathtaking sunset over the horizon. Enjoy complimentary drinks and snacks onboard while soaking in the serene atmosphere.

Duration: 2 hours

Difficulty: Easy

Price: $50 per person

Includes:Cruise, drinks, snacks

Trip: Jungle Trecing

Description: Explore the lush jungles surrounding the Nile River on our Jungle Trekking adventure. Follow experienced guides through hidden trails, encountering diverse flora and fauna along the way. Discover hidden waterfalls, ancient ruins, and stunning viewpoints.

Duration: Half day

Difficulty: Moderate

Price:$80 per person

Includes: Guided trek, snacks,water
